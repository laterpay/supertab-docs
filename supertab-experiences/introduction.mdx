---
title: Get Started
description: Monetize premium content using Supertab Experiences
---

Supertab Experiences is a drop in paywall solution which handles customer purchasing, billing
and access entitlement to your premium content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your inventory">
    Tell us about your <a href="./sites">Websites</a> and the <a href="./products-offerings-pricing">Products</a> you wish to sell.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your experiences">
    <a href="./experiences">Experiences</a> control which products will be offered to your customers.
    The Experience Editor automatically generates code to include on your Website.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deploy Supertab.js on pages you wish to monetize">
    Include the provided code snippet on pages you want to monetize with a paywall.
    <a href="/supertab-js">Supertab.js</a> handles all user interaction and reports back to you when a customer has purchased an entitlement.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sell premium content">
    Supertab manages your customers' purchases and payments so that you can focus on what you do best.
  </Step>
</Steps>
